About

Undergraduate in Physics at SUSTech, Class of 2028

This site is built as a working laboratory: a place to turn papers, calculations, spectra, and half-formed questions into durable notes.

github x mathstack

I am an undergraduate in physics at SUSTech, interested in experimental condensed matter physics, quantum materials, topology, and the mathematical habits that make complicated ideas readable.

The site is intentionally built like a lab notebook rather than a publication list. It should make the path from definition to measurement easier to follow.

Principle

Make every claim traceable to either a definition, a measurement, or a model assumption.

Principle

Prefer compact derivations over compressed conclusions.

Principle

Use design to make hard material more navigable, not louder.

Supervision

Condensed-matter training at SUSTech.

Current interests center on novel quantum phases, topological materials, and experimental evidence that connects spectroscopy with transport.
